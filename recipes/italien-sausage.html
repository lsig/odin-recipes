Ingredients

  • Italian sausage: 1 sausage
  • Onions: 2 medium, peeled and sliced
  • Red bell pepper: 1, cut into strips

Steps

  1. Cook sausage

    2. Heat a cast iron skillet over medium-high heat. Add sausage and cook, flipping every few minutes, until golden brown and an instant-read thermometer inserted into the center reads at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C), about 10 minutes.

  2. Serve

    3. Transfer sausage to a plate, leaving juices behind in the skillet. Add onions and red bell pepper; cook and stir until brown and tender, 5 to 10 minutes.
