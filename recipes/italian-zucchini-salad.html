Ingredients

  • Garlic: 4 cloves, crushed
  • Dried rosemary: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Sea salt
  • Black pepper
  • Zucchini: 2 diced
  • Hard italian salami: 4 ounce, diced
  • Almonds: 12, chopped
  • Olive oil: 1/3 cup
  • Balsamic vinegar: 1 dash
  • Mozzarella

Steps

  1. Mix garlic, rosemary, sea salt and pepper together in a large bowl
  2. Add zucchini, salami, mozzarella and almonds
  3. Mix well
  4. Stir in olive oil and balsamic vinegar
  5. Let stand for 10 minutes
  6. Serve
