The Best Lasagna

This is my grandma's lasagna recipe which every one in my family loves. If you want a yummy lasagna you have to try this recipe. Seriously I mean it. As most italien recipes the key is to have mostly fresh ingredients, it makes the dish so much better, I promise.

What makes this lasagna truly great is that it is always made with love, which is the most important ingredient in any italian dish. Also the second most important ingredient, is you guessed it; cheese. All kinds of cheese, I mean who doesn't love cheese. If you don't like cheese I would click away immedietly.
